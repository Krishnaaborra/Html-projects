The Khalistan movement is a separatist movement seeking to create a homeland for Sikhs by establishing a sovereign state called Khalistan (Punjabi: ਖ਼ਾਲਿਸਤਾਨ, lit. 'land of the Khalsa') in the Punjab region.[2] The proposed boundaries of Khalistan vary between different groups; some suggest the entirety of Indian Punjab, while others advocate only parts of it be ceded. Larger claims include Pakistani Punjab and other parts of North India such as Chandigarh.[3] Shimla[4] and Lahore[5] have been proposed as Khalistan's capital.
